## FAQ

### Q. The app is not monitoring my website.
Please check that the URL is entered correctly, starting with `https://` or `http://`. Also, make sure your device has an active internet connection.

### Q. Notifications are not working.
Go to your device's **Settings > Notifications > Network Watch** and make sure notifications are enabled. Also check the monitoring interval in the app settings.

### Q. How do I restore my premium purchase?
Go to **Settings > Premium** in the app and tap **Restore Purchases**. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

### Q. My data is not syncing between devices.
Make sure iCloud is enabled on all devices: **Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Network Watch**. Both devices must be connected to the internet.

### Q. How do I remove ads?
You can remove all ads by purchasing Network Watch Premium in **Settings > Premium**.
